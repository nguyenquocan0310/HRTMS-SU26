# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Administrators and race-operations staff use HRTMS in an internal workspace to manage tournaments, race entries, entry fees, and race officials. They work through repeated operational tasks and need current state, available actions, and errors to be immediately understandable.

## Product Purpose

HRTMS coordinates horse-racing tournament operations from registration and approvals through race allocation, fee confirmation, official assignment, and live-race preparation. Success means an Admin can complete each workflow quickly, understand why an action is unavailable, and trust that the screen reflects the backend state.

## Brand Personality

Clear, dependable, operational.

The interface should feel calm and precise during repeated administrative work, with direct labels, explicit status transitions, and useful feedback after every action.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative marketing layouts, ambiguous status colors, silent empty states, hidden API failures, and controls that appear available when business rules will reject them.

## Design Principles

- Show the operational state that determines the next valid action.
- Keep backend rules visible through clear labels, disabled states, and actionable errors.
- Optimize repeated workflows for scanning and low-friction confirmation.
- Treat empty, loading, and failure states as first-class parts of the workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, semantic controls, visible focus states, descriptive labels, and clear loading or disabled states. Do not rely on color alone to communicate status. Support reduced motion by keeping feedback functional without requiring animation.
